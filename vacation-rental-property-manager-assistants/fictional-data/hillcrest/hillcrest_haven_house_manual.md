## House Rules

1. **No smoking** inside or on the covered porch. Smoking is permitted in the backyard only. Please dispose of all cigarette butts properly.
2. **No parties or events.** Maximum occupancy is 8 guests.
3. **Pets:** Up to 2 well-behaved dogs are welcome. Please do not leave pets unattended inside. A $75 pet fee has been applied to your reservation. Clean up after your pet in the yard.
4. **Quiet hours:** 10:00 PM – 8:00 AM. Please be mindful of neighbors.
5. **No shoes inside.** There's a shoe rack by the front door.
6. **Fireplace:** The gas fireplace in the living room is for use October–April only. Use the remote on the mantel. Never leave it unattended.
7. **No furniture rearranging.** Please do not move the large furniture pieces.
8. **Trash & recycling:** Blue bin = recycling, green bin = trash. Pickup is Tuesday morning. Bins are in the side yard.
9. **Dishes:** Please rinse and load the dishwasher before checkout. You don't need to run it.
10. **Towels/linens:** Provided towels are for indoor use only. Beach/lake towels are in the hall closet.

Violation of house rules may result in forfeiture of security deposit.

## Around the House

### Thermostat
The Nest thermostat is in the hallway. Set to your preferred temperature. Please set to 65°F when you leave.

### Hot Water
The water heater is a 50-gallon tank. If you have a large group, give it 20–30 minutes between long showers.

### Garbage Disposal
Switch is under the left side of the sink. Run cold water before and during use.

### Propane Grill
Tank is on the side of the house. Turn the valve counterclockwise to open. Light with the igniter button on the front. **Never leave unattended.**

### Fire Pit
Wood is stacked under the back porch. Please use only the provided wood. Never burn trash. Always fully extinguish before going inside — there's a bucket and sand kit by the pit.
