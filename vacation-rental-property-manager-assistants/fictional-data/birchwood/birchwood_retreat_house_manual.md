10. **Hot tub:** Please shower before entering. Limit: 4 people at a time. Do not use glassware in or near the hot tub. Do not adjust chemical settings — these are maintained weekly by our service provider.

## Wood-Burning Fireplace — Important Instructions

This is a wood-burning fireplace, not gas. Please follow these instructions carefully.

1. **Before lighting:** Check that the damper is fully open. The damper handle is inside the firebox on the upper left. Pull it toward you to open. You should feel airflow.
2. **Priming the flue:** If the cabin has been cold, the flue may be cold and can backdraft. Hold a lit rolled newspaper near the open damper for 30 seconds before building your fire to warm the flue.
3. **Building the fire:** Place 2–3 small logs on the grate. Crumple newspaper underneath. Add a few kindling sticks. Light the newspaper from below.
4. **Maintain:** Add one log at a time once the fire is established. Never overfill the firebox.
5. **Extinguishing:** Never leave a fire unattended overnight or when leaving the cabin. Use the fireplace tools to spread out embers. Allow to cool completely before closing the damper.
6. **Firewood:** Stacked on the south side of the cabin. The bin on the hearth holds enough for 1–2 evenings. Please re-stock it from the woodpile if you use it.

> ⚠️ **Do not burn trash, pine cones, or any material other than the provided firewood.**
